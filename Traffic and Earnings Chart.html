Column Explanations

  • Traffic (Monthly): The number of visitors your site receives each month.
  • Ad Earnings (CPM $3): Revenue from ads based on a $3 CPM (Cost Per 1,000 impressions).
  • Affiliate Earnings (Est.): Income from affiliate marketing (sales through referral links).
  • Sponsored Posts (Est.): Payments received for promotional posts published on your site.
  • Total Earnings Range: The combined income from ads, affiliates, and sponsored posts.
