Explanation of Data

Traffic (Monthly): The number of unique visitors your website receives each month.

Ad Earnings (CPM $3): Revenue generated from ads, based on a $3 cost per 1,000 impressions.

Affiliate Earnings (Est.): Income earned through affiliate links when visitors purchase products or services.

Sponsored Posts (Est.): Payments received for publishing promotional content on your website.

Total Earnings Range: The combined revenue from ads, affiliate sales, and sponsored posts.
